# Execution Plan: Pay Software Subscription

## Overview
Detailed plan to pay $250 software subscription fee.

## Step 1: Verify Vendor Information
- Confirm vendor name and contact details
- Verify subscription service details
- Check billing cycle and renewal date
- Ensure payment is due at this time

## Step 2: Prepare Payment Documentation
- Document payment reason and service received
- Verify amount is correct
- Check budget allocation for this expense
- Prepare payment reference information

## Step 3: Review Company Policy
- According to company rules, payments above $100 require approval
- Amount of $250 requires formal approval
- Prepare approval request with justification

## Step 4: Submit for Approval
- Create approval request in Pending_Approval folder
- Include vendor information and payment justification
- Specify urgency level if renewal is time-sensitive

## Step 5: Execute Payment After Approval
- Process payment through approved channels
- Obtain payment confirmation
- Save payment receipt and documentation

## Step 6: Update Records
- Log payment in system
- Update budget/expenses tracking
- Notify relevant stakeholders
- Close out the task

## Timeline
- Day 1: Complete steps 1-2 (verification and preparation)
- Day 1: Complete step 3-4 (submit for approval)
- Upon approval: Complete steps 5-6 (execute and update records)
